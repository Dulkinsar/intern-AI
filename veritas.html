What Is a Startup?

A startup is a young company designed to solve problems through innovative technology and business models.

Key Characteristics:

  • Fast-growing - Aims to scale rapidly
  • Technology-driven - Uses cutting-edge tools and platforms
  • Problem-solving - Addresses real market needs
  • Risk-taking - Willing to experiment and pivot
  • Impact-focused - Seeks to create meaningful change

Examples you know: Kaspi, Yandex, Airbnb, Uber, Instagram

Startups don't just build products - they build the future

Why Startups Matter to You

Career Opportunities:

  • High growth potential - Join companies that scale 10x-100x
  • Diverse skill development - Wear multiple hats, learn fast
  • Innovation exposure - Work with latest technologies
  • Global impact - Reach millions of users worldwide

For Kazakhstan:

  • Economic diversification - Beyond oil and mining
  • Job creation - New industries, new opportunities
  • International competitiveness - Competing on global stage
  • Youth empowerment - Leading technological transformation
The next generation of Kazakh leaders will come from startups

  • Digital marketing - SEO, social media, content creation

How to Start Now

While Still in School:

  • Learn by doing - Build small projects, websites, apps
  • Join communities - Tech meetups, online forums, hackathons
  • Find mentors - Connect with professionals in your field
  • Gain experience - Internships, part-time work, volunteering

Post-Graduation Paths:

  • Join existing startups - Learn from experienced teams
  • Start your own - With co-founders and clear vision
  • Corporate innovation - Intrapreneurship in large companies
  • Further education - Specialized degrees or certifications
The best time to start is now - every expert was once a beginner
