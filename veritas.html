02/10

The Problem

  • Students don't know how to get real internships
  • Companies don't trust school-age resumes
  • No reliable portfolio or feedback system
  • Students with disabilities excluded from opportunity
  • Career progress with no social growth leads to shallow outcomes

The Solution

Intern AI is not just a platform — it's a full-stack learning & matching system with AI at its core:

  • Adaptive track recommendations
  • Behavioral scoring & internship readiness prediction
  • Portfolio quality evaluation
  • Mentor-student matchmaking
  • Verifiable AI-backed certification
  • Social mission integration

Deep Tech Stack

📈 Readiness Prediction

Behavioral signals analyzed to forecast internship success probability

🧾 Portfolio Scoring

Algorithmic scoring of projects + clarity of work

🔁 Feedback Loops

✅ Explainable Certification
